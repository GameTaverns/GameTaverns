# Game Tavern — Positioning Pivot

## The Shift

**FROM:** "A board game collection manager" (competing with BGG)  
**TO:** "The club & convention platform for tabletop gaming" (competing with nobody)

---

## Core Identity

Game Tavern is where **organized play happens**. We're the platform for board game clubs, convention lending desks, café game nights, and any group that brings people together around a table.

Collection management still exists — it's the engine underneath — but it's not what we lead with. Nobody wakes up thinking "I need a better spreadsheet for my games." They wake up thinking "I need to organize game night" or "I need to track 200 checkouts at GenCon."

---

## Who We Serve (Priority Order)

### 1. Convention & Event Organizers
Organizations like LoveThyNerd who run lending libraries at conventions. They need:
- Real-time checkout/return tracking across multiple lending libraries
- Staff-managed lending desks with barcode scanning
- Guest walk-up support (no account required for borrowers)
- Inventory visibility across the event
- Condition tracking and damage reporting

### 2. Board Game Clubs & Groups
Local groups, meetup organizers, game café regulars. They need:
- A shared space for their group (not a Discord server or Facebook group)
- Event scheduling and coordination
- Shared game catalog across members ("who owns Brass?")
- Lending between members with tracking
- Forums and social interaction

### 3. Board Game Cafés (B2B)
Cafés and retail spaces with play-and-borrow libraries. They need:
- Inventory management for their lending collection
- Customer-facing catalog (embed widget, kiosk mode)
- Play tracking and popular game analytics
- Event promotion and community building

### 4. Enthusiast Collectors (Secondary)
Individual collectors who want more than BGG. They get:
- Collection intelligence (value tracking, DNA analysis, archetypes)
- Play logging and stats
- Social features (follows, activity feed)
- But this is NOT the lead message anymore

## What Changes in the Product

### Navigation / IA
- "Clubs" and "Events" should be more prominent than "My Library"
- Dashboard should lead with social/club activity, not collection stats
- Onboarding should ask "Are you joining a club?" before "Add your games"

### Marketing Pages
- Landing page complete rewrite (per above)
- Grow/referral page → reframe around growing your club
- SEO → target "board game club app", "convention game lending", "game night organizer"

### Features to Prioritize
1. Club lending polish (already mostly built)
2. Convention lending desk enhancements
3. Event discovery and coordination
4. Club onboarding flow
5. Convention Game Concierge (reservation/spinner — when inventory question is resolved)

### Features to De-emphasize
- Collection-first messaging
- "BGG alternative" positioning
- Individual catalog features in marketing (keep building them, just don't lead with them)
